— How a pump-out goes

Four steps, start to finish.

No mystery, no upsell. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you call to the moment we pull out of the driveway.

01

Call & schedule

Tell us your address and roughly when the tank was last pumped. We'll book you into a same-week slot and call ahead before we arrive.

02

Locate & uncover

We find the tank, expose the lids, and check sludge levels, baffles and overall condition as we open it up.

03

Pump & clean

We vacuum out every bit of solids and liquid, then rinse the tank down so it's genuinely empty — not just skimmed off the top.

04

Inspect & advise

We check the filter and outlet, button everything back up, and tell you the real date you'll be due again. Yard left clean.
